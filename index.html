About Tubod

Tubod, officially the Municipality of Tubod, is a 2nd class municipality and capital of the province of Lanao del Norte, Philippines. According to the 2020 census, it has a population of 60,182 people.

Tubod derived its name from the spring located at Sitio Baybay in Barangay Poblacion. According to the old folks, even during the longest dry season, the spring will not drain. Tubod comes from the Cebuano language, meaning “the water source”. History pointed out that Christian immigrants from Luzon and Visayas settled in this part of the region.

Attractions

Kalilangan Cold Spring

The crystal-clear waters of this spring are a sight to behold, inviting visitors to immerse themselves in its tranquil beauty.

Mindanao Civic Center

Mindanao Civic Center (MCC) is a multimillion sports complex located in the town of Tubod—the capital town of Lanao del Norte.

Dapit Alim Shrine

Dapit Alim is one of the famous Shrines in Tubod, Lanao del Norte. It’s a great place to relax, especially when you need peace of mind.

Panguil Bay Bridge

Panguil Bay Bridge is an under-construction 3.77 km (2.34 mi) bridge connecting Tangub, Misamis Occidental to Tubod, Lanao del Norte.
